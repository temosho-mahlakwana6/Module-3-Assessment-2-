Chicken Wings

The Price: R120,00

Serving Size: 10 wings

About the starters

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a baking sheet with foil and place a cooling rack on top of the foil.
  2. Stir together flour, salt, and black pepper. Pat wings dry with paper towels. Brush with oil, then toss in the flour...
  3. Bake for 15 minutes, then broil for 2-3 minutes until crispy. WATCH CLOSELY so they don't burn.
