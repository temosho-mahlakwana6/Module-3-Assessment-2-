Maitai

The Price: R40,00

Serving Size: large cup

About the Drink

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker about halfway with crushed ice.
  2. Add the rum, lime juice, orange liqueur, orgeat, and simple syrup. Cover, and shake well, about 30 seconds.
  3. Pour equally into two old-fashioned glasses; top with more crushed ice as needed. Garnish with mint springs and lime, and serve.
